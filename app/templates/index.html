Welcome!

This product displays data on the air quality in NYC from 2005 to 2021. The data is taken from Data.gov and contains information such as indicator/pollutant name,
their unit of measurement, geographical location it was measured at, and the date the data applies to. A sample of 50 records from the cleaned dataset are displayed for easier viewing.


Link to dataset: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/air-quality



Refer to the links in the menu above to view the data, data dictionary, or login to add changes to the dataset.






References:

Data.gov. (2023, November 3). Air quality. https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/air-quality
NYC Open Data. (2023, November 2). Air quality. https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Environment/Air-Quality/c3uy-2p5r
